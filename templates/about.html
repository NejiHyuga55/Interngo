Our Story & Mission

At InternGo, we believe that every student deserves a chance to find the perfect internship that aligns with their passion and potential. Our journey began with a simple idea: to remove the friction from the internship search process using technology and a deep commitment to India's youth.

Who We Are

InternGo is a dedicated platform operating under the **Prime Minister Internship Scheme**. We are a team of technologists and career experts committed to bridging the gap between talent and opportunity. Our goal is to create a transparent and efficient ecosystem where students can easily find and apply for government and private sector internships, while organizations can discover bright, motivated individuals.

Our Vision

To be the leading AI-powered platform for student internships in India, empowering millions of young people to gain invaluable work experience, grow their professional networks, and contribute to the nation's development.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We use cutting-edge AI technology to provide smarter, more personalized internship recommendations.

Empowerment

Our platform is built to empower students with the tools and information they need to succeed.

Integrity

We ensure a secure, fair, and transparent process for both students and internship providers.

Community

We foster a community of learning and growth, connecting students with mentors and opportunities.
