Eligibility Criteria

To participate in the Prime Minister Internship Scheme, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Currently enrolled in a recognized university or recently graduated (within last 2 years)
  • Between 18 to 25 years of age
  • Minimum 60% marks or equivalent CGPA in last qualifying examination
  • Indian citizenship with valid government ID proof
  • No prior criminal record or ongoing legal proceedings
Ongoing! Check your dashboard, email, and SMS regularly. Confirm joining via the Internship tile on your dashboard.
