Eligibility Criteria

To participate in the Prime Minister Internship Scheme, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Between 18 to 24 years of age
  • Matriculation + ITI in relevant trade
  • intermediate + AICTE -recognized diploma
  • No prior criminal record or ongoing legal proceedings

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Prime Minister Internship Scheme? +
The Prime Minister Internship Scheme is a government initiative designed to provide valuable work experience to young graduates and diploma holders across various sectors in India. It aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical industry requirements.
Who is eligible to apply for the internship? +
Applicants must be between 18-24 years of age and hold either a Bachelor's degree from a UGC/AICTE-recognized institution, a relevant ITI trade certificate after matriculation, or an AICTE-recognized diploma after intermediate education.
How does the AI recommendation system work? +
Our AI system analyzes your skills, educational background, interests, and location preferences to match you with the most suitable internship opportunities from our partner companies. The algorithm considers multiple factors to provide personalized recommendations.
Is there any financial assistance provided? +
Yes, selected candidates receive a stipend during their internship period. The amount varies based on the role, location, and duration of the internship. Additional financial support may be available for candidates from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.
How long does the internship last? +
Internship durations typically range from 3 to 6 months, depending on the specific role and company requirements. Some specialized positions may have different durations.
Can I apply if I'm currently studying? +
The program is primarily designed for those who have completed their education. However, final year students may be eligible for certain positions. Please check specific eligibility requirements for each internship opportunity.
What documents are required for registration? +
You will need your educational certificates, identity proof (Aadhaar card, passport, or driver's license), address proof, and a recent photograph. Specific internships may require additional documents.
